# Wedding Car Pricing Reference

## Standard Australian Wedding Car Hire Rates (2024-2026)

### Package Options:

**4-Hour Package** (Most Popular)
- Mercedes-Benz S-Class: $450-$550
- BMW 7 Series: $450-$550
- Luxury SUV: $400-$500
- Includes: Chauffeur, ribbons, champagne, red carpet

**6-Hour Package**
- Mercedes-Benz S-Class: $650-$750
- BMW 7 Series: $650-$750
- Luxury SUV: $600-$700

**8-Hour Package** (Full Day)
- Mercedes-Benz S-Class: $850-$950
- BMW 7 Series: $850-$950
- Luxury SUV: $800-$900

**Bridal Party Package**
- Mercedes V-Class (7 passengers): $500-$600 (4 hours)
- Multiple vehicles: Custom quote

### What's Included:
✅ Professional chauffeur in formal attire
✅ Red carpet service
✅ White satin ribbons & tulle
✅ Champagne on ice
✅ Unlimited photos with vehicle
✅ Complimentary water & mints
✅ Flexible timing (minor delays included)

### Add-Ons:
- Custom ribbon colors: $50
- Additional hour: $120-$150
- Vintage/Classic car: +$200-$300
- Bridal party transport: From $500

### Peak Season (Sep-May):
- Book 6-12 months in advance
- Prices may increase 10-15% for peak dates
- Saturday weddings most popular
