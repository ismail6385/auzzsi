   - Answer: Tipping is not mandatory in Australia, but it's appreciated for exceptional service. If you choose to tip your chauffeur, 10-15% is customary for outstanding service.

3. **How early should I book an airport transfer?**
   - Answer: We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for guaranteed availability. However, Auzzie Chauffeur accepts last-minute bookings subject to vehicle availability, even with just 2 hours' notice.

4. **What's the difference between a taxi and airport transfer?**
   - Answer: Airport transfers offer fixed pricing, flight tracking, complimentary wait time, and meet & greet service. Taxis use meters with variable pricing and don't include these premium services.

5. **Can I book an airport transfer for someone else?**
   - Answer: Yes! You can book and pay for an airport transfer for family, friends, or business colleagues. Simply provide their flight details and contact information during booking.

6. **What happens if my flight is delayed?**

7. **How do I find my chauffeur at the airport?**
   - Answer: Your chauffeur will wait at the arrivals area holding a name sign with your name. You'll receive their contact details and photo via SMS before your flight lands.

8. **Is luggage assistance included?**
   - Answer: Yes, all airport transfers include complimentary luggage assistance. Your chauffeur will help load and unload your bags.
