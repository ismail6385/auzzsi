# Internal Linking Strategy - Auzzie Chauffeur Website

## Overview
Every page with 2000+ words should have **10-12 internal links** naturally distributed throughout the content.

## Link Distribution Formula (Per 2000-word Page)

### 1. **Primary Keywords (2-3 links)**
- Branded: "Auzzie Chauffeur", "Auzzie Chauffeur Service"
- Core Service: "chauffeur service", "airport transfers", "corporate travel"
- **Placement**: Introduction (first 300 words)

### 2. **LSI / Longtail Keywords (6-8 links)**
- Service variants: "private driver", "executive transport", "luxury car hire"
- Location-specific: "chauffeur Melbourne", "Sydney airport transfer"
- Service combinations: "corporate airport transfer", "wedding car service"
- **Placement**: Main content sections (distributed across 1200 words)

### 3. **Branded CTAs (1-2 links)**
- "Book now", "Get a quote", "View our fleet"
- "Contact us", "Learn more about our services"
- **Placement**: Conclusion or CTA sections (last 300 words)

## Implementation Guidelines

### **Best Practices:**
1. **Natural Flow**: Links should feel organic, not forced
2. **Anchor Text Variety**: Don't repeat same anchor text multiple times
3. **Relevant Context**: Link only when it adds value to the reader
4. **Distribution**: Spread links throughout content, not clustered
5. **Deep Linking**: Link to specific pages, not just homepage
6. **User Intent**: Consider what user might want to explore next

### **Avoid:**
- ‚ùå Over-optimization (too many exact-match keywords)
- ‚ùå Irrelevant links (linking just for SEO)
- ‚ùå Broken link chains (linking to pages that don't exist)
- ‚ùå Same anchor text repetition
- ‚ùå Clustering all links in one paragraph

### **Link Styling:**
- Color: Navy blue (#1e3a8a or #1a2b4b)
- Decoration: Gold underline (#c5a467)
- Hover: Gold background with white text
- Font-weight: 600 (semi-bold)

## Tracking & Maintenance

### **Monthly Review:**
- Check for broken internal links
- Update anchor text if page titles change
- Add new service/location pages to linking strategy
- Monitor which internal links get most clicks (via analytics)

### **Priority Pages for Internal Linking:**
1. Homepage (highest authority)
2. Service pages (conversion-focused)
3. Location hubs (local SEO)
4. Fleet pages (product showcase)
5. About pages (trust-building)
